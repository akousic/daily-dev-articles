## Summary

InfoQ has opened enrollment for three five-week online certification cohorts starting in August: Architecture with Luca Mezzalira, starting August 13; Engineering Leadership with Michelle Brush, starting August 21; and AI Security & Privacy Engineering with Katharine Jarmul, starting August 26. Each cohort is limited to practitioners with at least five years of experience, and live sessions run for four hours a week. Senior engineers and architects making architecture, leadership, or AI security decisions are often the most senior person in the room, with few outside voices to check their thinking.

## Key Takeaways

- Each week, participants take a framework from a QCon talk and apply it to a real decision from their own work, then discuss what worked, what failed, and what's still uncertain with a confidential peer group of senior engineers and architects from other companies.
- Architecture, with Luca Mezzalira (starts August 13) Luca Mezzalira is the author of Building Micro-Frontends (O'Reilly) and creator of the "Dear Architects" newsletter.
- His cohort, the InfoQ Certified Software Architect in Emerging Technologies program, covers the sociotechnical side of the job: trade-offs and how to communicate them, decentralized decision-making, platform engineering, and architecture decisions for AI systems.
