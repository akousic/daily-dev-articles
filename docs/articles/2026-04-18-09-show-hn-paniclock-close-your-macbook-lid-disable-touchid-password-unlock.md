## Summary

PanicLock is macOS menu bar utility that instantly disables Touch ID and locks the screen with a single click or closing your laptop lid. PanicLock fills a gap macOS leaves open: there is no built-in way to instantly disable Touch ID when it matters. Biometrics are convenient day-to-day, and sometimes preferable when you need speed or want to avoid your password being observed.

## Key Takeaways

- But in sensitive situations, law enforcement and border agents in many countries can compel a biometric unlock in ways they cannot with a password.
