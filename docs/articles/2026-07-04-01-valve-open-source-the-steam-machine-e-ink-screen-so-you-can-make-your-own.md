## Summary

While Valve will not be making and providing their own e-ink display for the Steam Machine, they have opened it up so anyone can now do it. Valve originally teased it with the first lot of reviewers that got their hands on it. All of it is available on their GitLab under the MIT license, which goes over everything you need to make your own and stick it on the front of your fancy new Steam Machine.

## Key Takeaways

- Image Credit - Gamers Nexus They're now calling it the "Inkterface" and there's a good few things you'll need to make it including: - 1 x Adafruit ESP32 Feather with 2MB PSRAM.

- - 1 x Adafruit 5.83" Monochrome eInk Panel.
