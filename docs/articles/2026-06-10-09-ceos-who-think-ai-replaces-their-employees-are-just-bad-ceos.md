CEOs Who Think AI Replaces Their Employees Are Just Bad CEOs from the that's-not-what-ai-is-for dept In the last three months I’ve had people forward me four separate examples of a CEO losing his or her mind over AI. What’s been striking to me is the similarity in each case: It would be an “all hands” email in which the CEO talks up how amazing LLM tools are and saying that everyone in the company MUST start learning to use them immediately or they should look for a job elsewhere. ## Key Takeaways

- Sometimes they are setting up “office hours” or internal “AI hackathons.” But in every case the gist is the same “holy shit AI is amazing and you are expected to use it at your job all the time.” The worst case of these were the few companies that set up token leaderboards, which is perhaps the dumbest way possible to encourage learning how to use LLMs well.
- Good usage of AI includes learning how to view tokens as a scarce resource.
