## Summary

You Need to Rewrite Your CLI for AI Agents - Human DX optimizes for discoverability and forgiveness. - Agent DX optimizes for predictability and defense-in-depth. - These are different enough that retrofitting a human-first CLI for agents is a losing bet.

## Key Takeaways

- I built a CLI for Google Workspace — agents first.
- Not “built a CLI, then noticed agents were using it.” From Day One, the design assumptions were shaped by the fact that AI agents would be the primary consumers of every command, every flag, and every byte of output.
- CLIs are increasingly the lowest-friction interface for AI agents to reach external systems.
