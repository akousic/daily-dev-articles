## Summary

We open our IDE and let a model running somewhere in the cloud read our entire codebase to add a null check - and track our behaviour along the way. We open Google Docs and ask Gemini to fix a typo. We fire up GPT-class models to refine a Slack message, restructure a comment, generate a thumbnail.

## Key Takeaways

- We're going to shove AI into every single hole that has data for it to be trained on.
- I'm not saying we shouldn't - that's the nature of expected technological progress, and there isn't much choice in the matter.
- But somewhere along the way we stopped asking whether the scale of the model matches the scale of the task.
