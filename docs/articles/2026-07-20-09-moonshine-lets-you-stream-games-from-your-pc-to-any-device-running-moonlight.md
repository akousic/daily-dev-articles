## Summary

Moonshine lets you stream games from your PC to any device running Moonlight. Your keyboard, mouse, and controller inputs are sent back to the host so you can play games remotely as if you were sitting in front of it. - Isolated streaming sessions: Each stream runs in its own compositor, completely separate from your desktop environment.

## Key Takeaways

- Your host PC can still be used for other things while you stream.
- - No monitor required: Works on headless servers — no HDMI dummy plug needed.
- - Hardware video encoding: H.264, H.265, and AV1 encoding using the GPU.
