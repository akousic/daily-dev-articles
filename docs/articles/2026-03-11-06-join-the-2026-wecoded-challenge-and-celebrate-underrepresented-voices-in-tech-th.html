We’re excited to announce the launch of the 2026 WeCoded Challenge! This year marks our ninth year celebrating the voices of underrepresented and marginalized developers on DEV. Our journey began in 2017 with Nevertheless, She Coded, a movement centered on solidarity and storytelling.
