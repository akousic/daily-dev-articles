## Summary

LinkedIn has introduced a unified integrations platform designed to standardize and reconcile hiring data across disparate systems, improving data quality, partner onboarding speed, and enabling downstream AI applications. The multi-year effort consolidates fragmented recruitment data pipelines into a consistent and scalable foundation for talent products and AI-driven features. Recruiting at LinkedIn operates at a significant scale, ingesting data from applicant tracking systems, career sites, and job boards.

## Key Takeaways

- These sources often produce inconsistent schemas and incomplete records, creating challenges for downstream analytics and product features.
