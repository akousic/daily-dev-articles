## Summary

Revenium has announced the general availability of its Tool Registry, a new capability designed to give enterprises a complete, end-to-end view of what their AI agents actually cost. Moving beyond traditional token-based tracking, the platform attributes every expense, across APIs, third-party services, and even human intervention, back to the specific AI decision that triggered it, providing what the company claims is the first full-stack view of agent-driven spend. While most organizations closely monitor large language model (LLM) token usage, Revenium argues that tokens represent only a fraction of the true cost of running AI agents.

## Key Takeaways

- In real-world workflows, agents routinely call external services, such as credit bureaus, identity verification platforms, fraud detection tools, and data providers, each incurring transaction-based fees that are often significantly higher than the cost of the model itself.
